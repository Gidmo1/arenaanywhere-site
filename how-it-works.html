How It Works

From download to your first match in 3 simple steps

Step 1

Download & Setup

Get the App

  1. Download ArenaAnywhere for Android
  2. Install on your device (Android 7.0 or higher)
  3. Open the app and sign in
  4. Choose your username (this is how friends find you)
  5. Grant necessary permissions (network access)
Pro Tip: Choose a memorable username - your friends will search for you with it!

Sign In Screen

Step 2

Find Your Opponent

Two Ways to Play

Quick Match

  • Tap "Quick Match" on home screen
  • We find an opponent at your skill level
  • Usually takes 5-15 seconds
  • Perfect for competitive play

Play with Friends

  • Go to "Search" tab
  • Search friend's username
  • Send friend request
  • Once accepted, challenge them anytime
Pro Tip: Your friend code is in your profile. Share it on WhatsApp to quickly add friends!

Find Opponent

Step 3

Connect & Play

Automatic Connection

  1. Once matched, both players enter the Match Lobby
  2. Tap "Connect to Network" button
  3. Our VPN connects you automatically (10 seconds)
  4. Your IP and opponent's IP show on screen
  5. Tap the copy button next to each IP

Configure PPSSPP

  1. Open PPSSPP → Settings → Networking
  2. Enable "Enable networking/WLAN"
  3. Host: Enable "Built-in PRO ad hoc server"
  4. Guest: Enter host's IP in "Change PRO ad hoc server IP"
  5. Both set Port offset to 10000
  6. Start eFootball → Ad-hoc → Host creates, Guest joins
You're Playing! Enjoy lag-free matches with minimal packet loss.
