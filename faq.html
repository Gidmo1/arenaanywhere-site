Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ArenaAnywhere

What is ArenaAnywhere?

ArenaAnywhere is a companion app for PPSSPP that makes online multiplayer simple. We handle VPN connections, matchmaking, and player coordination so you can focus on playing.

How much does it cost?

ArenaAnywhere offers a free tier with all core features. We may introduce optional premium features in the future for advanced functionality.

Which games does it support?

ArenaAnywhere works with any PPSSPP game that has ad-hoc multiplayer support. It's optimized for eFootball/PES games, but also works with games like Monster Hunter, Tekken, and more.

Do I need other VPN apps?

No! ArenaAnywhere handles all VPN connections automatically through our servers. You don't need to install any additional VPN apps.

What are the minimum requirements?

Android 7.0 or higher, stable internet connection (WiFi or 4G), and PPSSPP emulator installed on your device.

Why can't I connect to my opponent?

Try these steps: 1) Check your internet connection, 2) Tap "Test Connection" in the match lobby, 3) Make sure both players have completed the connection step, 4) Restart the app if needed.

How do I configure PPSSPP?

Go to PPSSPP Settings → Networking → Enable "WLAN". Host players enable "Built-in PRO ad hoc server". Guest players enter the host's IP. Both players set Port offset to 10000. See our How It Works page for detailed instructions.

Is my connection secure?

Yes! All connections are encrypted end-to-end. Your real IP address is never exposed to other players. We use industry-standard VPN technology.

How does matchmaking work?

Our algorithm matches you with players of similar skill rating. You can also use Quick Match for instant games or challenge specific friends directly.

Can I play with friends in other countries?

Absolutely! ArenaAnywhere works globally. Our VPN technology ensures smooth connections regardless of location.

What is the skill rating system?

We use an ELO-based system. Win matches to gain rating, lose to lose points. Your rating determines matchmaking and leaderboard position.

How do tournaments work?

Tournaments run on weekends with bracket-style elimination. Sign up during the registration window, compete in your matches, and climb the bracket to win!

How do I create an account?

Simply sign in through the app. Choose your username and you're ready to play!

Can I change my username?

Yes, go to Settings → Edit Profile. Note that you can only change your username once every 30 days.

How do I add friends?

Go to the Search tab, enter their username, and send a friend request. They can also add you using your unique friend code found in your profile.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
